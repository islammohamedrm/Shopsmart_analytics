# Shopsmart_analytics
End-to-end data analyst project featuring a synthetic e-commerce dataset — covers data cleaning, SQL joins &amp; aggregations, and Power BI visualization across 800 customers, 10,000 orders, and 5 product categories.

📌 Project Overview

Designed and generated a realistic synthetic e-commerce dataset simulating 2 years of business operations (2022–2024)
Modeled a star-schema-style data structure across 5 related tables with intentional dirty data for cleaning practice
Addressed 20 real business objectives framed as CEO-level questions across 4 analytical domains

📂 Dataset

800 customers across 30 US cities and 5 regions
10,000 orders — averaging 13.7 orders/day and 12.5 orders per customer
45 products across 5 categories supplied by 12 international suppliers
750 returns — representing a realistic 7.5% return rate
Intentionally injected dirty records including duplicate IDs, invalid emails, fake phone numbers, impossible ages, inconsistent casing, orphan orders, and invalid ship dates


🔍 SQL Analysis

Wrote 21 SQL queries ranging from beginner to advanced
Topics covered: GROUP BY aggregations, INNER/LEFT JOINs across 4 tables, HAVING filters, date functions, subqueries, CTEs, and window functions (RANK, LAG, PARTITION BY)
Key analyses: revenue by region, customer segmentation, product return rates, supplier scorecards, and month-over-month growth
